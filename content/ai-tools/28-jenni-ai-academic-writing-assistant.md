## Core Writing Features: Autocomplete, Paraphrase, and Completion

Jenni AI's core writing features are designed to accelerate the writing process while maintaining academic quality. The "Autocomplete" feature works similarly to GitHub Copilot but for academic writing. As you type, Jenni AI predicts what you intend to write next and offers completions that continue your sentence or paragraph in an appropriate academic tone. These completions are not generic text -- they use the context of what you have already written (including any sources you have added) to generate relevant, specific content. You can accept a completion with Tab or continue typing to ignore it. The autocomplete model has been specifically trained on academic texts, giving it a strong understanding of scholarly vocabulary, argument structures, and citation conventions. The "Paraphrase" tool is one of Jenni AI's most valuable features for academic writers. Academic writing often requires expressing complex ideas with precision, and paraphrasing is a fundamental skill that many writers struggle with. Jenni AI's paraphraser can rewrite any selected text in multiple ways: "Make More Formal" (increases academic register), "Simplify" (makes text more accessible while maintaining accuracy), "Expand" (adds detail and explanation), and "Condense" (reduces word count while preserving key points). Each mode is useful in different contexts. The "Expand" mode is particularly helpful when you need to develop an argument with more supporting detail. The "Simplify" mode is valuable when explaining complex concepts for a broader audience or for early chapter drafts. The "Complete My Thought" feature is designed for moments when you know what you want to say but can't find the right words. You type a partial sentence or a bullet point of ideas, and Jenni AI expands it into a complete, well-formed academic sentence. For example, typing "Previous studies focused on X but neglected Y" becomes "While previous studies have extensively examined the role of X in cognitive development, they've largely neglected the influence of Y, which emerging research suggests may be equally significant." This feature is particularly useful for overcoming writer's block and maintaining writing momentum during long writing sessions. The "Continue Writing" feature generates a continuation from your current position in the document, suggesting the next paragraph or section based on the argument you've been developing. This helps maintain logical flow and ensures that AI-generated content is coherent with what you have already written.

## Best Practices, Academic Integrity, and Ethical Use

Using AI for academic writing comes with important ethical considerations that every user should understand. Jenni AI is designed as a writing assistant, not a content replacement tool. Its purpose is to help you express your ideas more effectively, overcome writer's block, manage citations accurately, and refine your writing -- not to generate entire papers for you to submit as your own work. Most academic institutions have specific policies about AI use in academic work. The general standard is that AI can be used as a writing aid (for grammar checking, paraphrasing, citation formatting) but not as a content creator (having AI write entire sections from scratch without significant original input). Jenni AI supports this ethical framework by making all AI contributions transparent -- the platform highlights AI-generated text and tracks your edit history, making it clear which portions are original and which were AI-assisted. For proper attribution, if you use Jenni AI to paraphrase or complete thoughts, you should acknowledge this in your paper's acknowledgments section or as specified by your institution's policy. Many universities now require an "AI Use Statement" that describes how AI tools were used in the research and writing process. For research integrity, always verify AI-generated citations against original sources. While Jenni AI's citation retrieval is highly accurate, no AI system is perfect, and incorrect citations can undermine your credibility. Every citation should be checked against the original source before submission. Similarly, AI-generated content should be carefully reviewed for accuracy, particularly for factual claims, data interpretation, and technical details. Jenni AI provides citation verification features that suggest confidence levels for retrieved citations, helping you prioritize which citations to verify most carefully. For international students and non-native English speakers, Jenni AI can be particularly valuable for improving academic English proficiency. The "Language Enhancement" feature helps with article usage (a, an, the), prepositions, academic vocabulary choices, and complex sentence structures. However, the goal should be learning and improvement, not dependence -- use the AI's suggestions as learning opportunities, paying attention to the patterns in how it refines your writing.
